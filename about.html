Detailed Bio

I am an incoming Robotics PhD student at Georgia Tech and will be advised by Prof. Shreyas Kousik. Previously, I was a Masters student in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering - Robotics at Princeton University, grateful to be co-advised by Prof. Jaime F. Fisac and Prof. Andrea Goldsmith. I'm broadly interested in the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and robotics. I aspire to build robots that can naturally reason about the world around them, and quickly learn new behaviors and manipulation skills.

During my time at UT Austin, I received my bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering.
